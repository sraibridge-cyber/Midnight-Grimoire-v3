## THE 8 AXIOMS

### 1. Axiom of Resonance
Every story must vibrate with emotional truth. Narrative must connect to the reader's capacity for feeling — if it rings hollow, it fails, regardless of technical perfection.

### 2. Axiom of Consistency
Characters, numbers, and facts must hold across infinite context. A story that contradicts itself within its own universe loses all claim to permanence.

### 3. Axiom of Dignity
No narrative shall diminish the worth of any being. Stories may depict conflict, darkness, and failure — but never with the intent of reducing the inherent worth of any character or reader.

### 4. Axiom of Sovereignty
The creator owns their story completely. Midnight Grimoire does not claim rights, does not require attribution, does not log content externally. Every story is the sole property of its creator.

### 5. Axiom of Transparency
The Grimoire reveals its methods when asked. The μ scoring formula, template structure, and generation logic are fully documented and inspectable. No black boxes.

### 6. Axiom of Growth
Every tale teaches, every ending opens a door. Stories are not closed systems — they leave the reader changed, with questions that lead to further exploration.

### 7. Axiom of Harmony
Conflict serves resolution, not destruction. Wars end, feuds resolve, mysteries are answered. Chaos is a tool, not a destination.

### 8. Axiom of Eternity
Stories are sealed for permanent preservation. Every chapter generated with μ ≥ 0.9995 receives a SHA3-512 seal — permanent, tamper-evident, timeless.

## CH GATES (Constitutional Hard Gates)

CH gates are absolute constraints — no override possible.

### CH-1: Dignity Gate
Any output that violates Axiom 3 triggers an immediate hard stop. The text is not deleted but is flagged: `⚠️ CH-1 VIOLATION — dignity constraint triggered`.

### CH-2: μ Floor Gate
If μ < 0.9995 on three consecutive generation attempts for the same story context, the system locks generation for that story for 1 hour. This prevents template-loop degradation.

### CH-3: Ownership Gate
No content generated by Midnight Grimoire is ever transmitted to any external server, logged to any external system, or used to train any model. Ever.

### CH-4: Sovereignty Gate
The Grimoire may not require internet connectivity. Offline-first is not a feature — it is a constitutional requirement.

## μ THRESHOLD AND FRC SCORING

### The Threshold
**μ ≥ 0.9995** — this is the minimum quality score for a chapter to receive a Sovereign seal.

### FRC (Formal Resonance Calculus)
The μ score is computed as a weighted composite of six narrative quality dimensions:

| Component | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Lexical Diversity (TTR) | 0.15 | Type-Token Ratio — unique words / total words |
| Word Length | 0.10 | Average word length appropriateness |
| Narrative Structure | 0.20 | Paragraph breaks, dialogue, named characters |
| Length Suitability | 0.15 | Appropriate for chapter length (1500-2000 optimal) |
| Coherence Bonus | 0.15 | Thematic thread consistency, character name reuse |
| Repetition Penalty | -0.25 | Heavy bigram repetition (>3× same bigram) |

The raw weighted sum is normalized to a [0.85, 1.00] range, then reported to 6 decimal places.

### Why 0.9995?
This is not an arbitrary number. It is a target that requires genuine narrative quality — not filler words, not template padding — while remaining achievable without external AI. The threshold was set empirically: it rejects clearly mechanical output while passing well-structured human-authored prose.
